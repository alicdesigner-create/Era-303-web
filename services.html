Full Carpet Cleaning

Deep steam cleaning using high-powered equipment that reaches the base of carpet fibers. We remove dirt, allergens, bacteria, and stains from homes and commercial spaces. Safe for children and pets. Results are guaranteed — if you're not satisfied, we'll come back.

What's Included

  • Pre-treatment of stains and high-traffic areas
  • Hot water extraction deep clean
  • Deodorizing treatment
  • Fast dry time — back to normal quickly

Tile & Grout / Concrete & Hard Surface Cleaning

We restore the original shine of your tile floors and seal the grout to prevent future staining. We also work on concrete and other hard surfaces for both residential and commercial properties. Our high-pressure cleaning process dislodges embedded grime that regular mopping simply cannot reach.

What's Included

  • High-pressure deep cleaning
  • Grout sealing for long-lasting protection
  • Concrete and hard surface treatment
  • Surface protection finish

Pet Odor Treatment

Specialized enzymatic treatment that neutralizes pet odors and eliminates stains at the source — not just masks them. Effective on carpets, upholstery, and hard floors. Our treatment breaks down the organic compounds responsible for persistent pet odors so they don't return.

What's Included

  • Odor source identification
  • Enzymatic treatment application
  • Deep extraction cleaning
  • Deodorizing finish coat

Odor Removal / Fog Ozone Machine

Industrial-grade ozone fogging for spaces with persistent odors from smoke, mold, moisture, or other sources. Ozone molecules reach every surface — including walls, ceilings, furniture, and HVAC systems — and eliminate odor-causing bacteria at the molecular level.

What's Included

  • Full-space ozone treatment
  • Fogging application to all surfaces
  • Post-treatment ventilation guidance
  • Suitable for smoke, mold, and moisture odors

VCT Strip & Wax

Professional stripping and waxing of Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) floors for offices, retail spaces, and commercial environments. We remove old wax buildup, deep clean the surface, and apply multiple coats of high-gloss wax for a showroom-quality finish that protects the floor and lasts.

What's Included

  • Complete strip of old wax finish
  • Deep mechanical scrub
  • Multiple coats of premium wax
  • High-gloss professional finish
